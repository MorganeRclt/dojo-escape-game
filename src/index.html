The treasure of President Coolidge

Passionate about scuba diving, you have travelled the world and mostly the seven seas since numerous years. While you are looking for your next diving, you found a mysterious article about the wreck of the SS President Coolidge, sunk during the World War II near the Vanuatu, in South Pacific Ocean. According to its author, in one of the boat's room, named the Lady Room, a treasure would be hidden.
You love adventure, so you pack your equipment and fly to Vanuatu.
You dive, found the wreck and begin to look for the Lady Room. You enter in what look like the captain's room. You want to go out, but the door you came in through is blocked !
You have to find another exit, quickly, because you have just enough oxygen in your bottle for the next 30 minutes !
  • You have 40 minutes to escape the boat or you'll loose
  • You'll have to find code, it can be letter or digits
  • You'll find items : each items is useful for one only enigma
  • If you can't resolve an enigma, you can use clues. Click on "?". There is two clues for each enigma, begin with the first one (the left "?")
